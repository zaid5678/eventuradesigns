Our Story

Studio Photo

Crafted with Care,
Styled with Intention

At Eventura, we believe every event is an adventure. That's why we don't just create signs and backdrops — we craft experiences. Whether you come to us with a clear vision or just a spark of an idea, we work with you to shape every detail. From inspiration to execution, we build custom mood boards so you can see your vision before it even begins.

Eventura Designs began the way most creative businesses do — with a single event, a few borrowed props, and a realisation that the details matter more than anyone gives them credit for. That first backdrop, built in a spare room with borrowed tools and borrowed time, led to a second, then a third, and eventually to a full studio practice dedicated to bespoke event styling.

We work primarily across the UK, though our work has travelled further when the occasion has called for it. Our clients range from couples planning their wedding ceremony to businesses hosting product launches and everything in between. What ties them together is a desire for something that feels considered — not hired out of a van and inflated in ten minutes.

We care about the craft of what we make. Every piece is designed and built specifically for the client who commissioned it. We don't have a warehouse of generic hire stock. We have a studio, a process, and a strong conviction that no two events should look the same.

When it works well — and it usually does — you walk into your venue and it looks exactly like the version you imagined. That moment is what we work for.

What We Stand For

Personalisation

Your brief drives everything we design. We don't start from a template — we start from a conversation about what matters to you.

Attention to Detail

The quality of a setup lives in the things most guests won't consciously notice but would immediately miss if they were absent.

Memorable Experiences

We measure success by what your guests remember. The backdrop should be part of the story they tell afterwards.
