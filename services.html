What We Offer

Every service we provide is built around your brief. We don't work from a fixed catalogue — we work from your ideas.

Floral & Botanical Backdrops

Our floral backdrops are constructed using a combination of preserved, dried, and artificial botanicals chosen for their longevity and richness. Each arrangement is assembled on-site to suit your venue's dimensions and palette. Whether you want a wall of ivory roses for a ceremony or a wild meadow-style arch for a garden party, we build it around your brief.

Balloon Installations

Far beyond the standard bunch tied to a chair, our balloon work is considered and architectural. Organic balloon columns, ceiling clusters, and full-scale garland arches are all built to your colour specification and venue scale. We use professional-grade latex and foil — nothing pre-inflated and shipped in a box.

Sequin & Shimmer Walls

A perennial favourite for birthday celebrations and evening receptions. Our sequin panels are available in gold, silver, rose gold, and champagne, and can be configured to cover any wall width. They catch ambient light with every movement in the room, creating a backdrop that looks as good in person as it does on camera.

Custom Printed Signage

Printed pieces designed and produced specifically for your event. Order of service boards, welcome signs, table plans, bar menus, and personalised name cards — all produced to a consistent visual standard. We'll work with your existing design direction or help you develop one from scratch. Formats include acrylic, foam board, and heavy card stock.
